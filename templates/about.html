About CosmicTeams

CosmicTeams is a platform designed to help gamers form teams, collaborate, and achieve their gaming goals together. Our mission is to create a vibrant community where players can showcase their skills and find like-minded teammates.

Swift Ness

Founder & Lead Developer

A passionate developer and gamer who created CosmicTeams to bring players together. With years of experience in both gaming and software development, SwiftNess built this platform to solve the challenges teams face when organizing and managing their activities.

Our Story

CosmicTeams began as a small project in 2023, born from the frustration of trying to organize gaming sessions with friends across different time zones and games. What started as a simple tool for tracking team availability evolved into a comprehensive platform for team management, skill tracking, and community building.

Today, CosmicTeams serves thousands of gamers worldwide, helping them form lasting connections and achieve their competitive goals. We continue to grow and improve based on feedback from our amazing community.

Our Vision

We believe that gaming is better together. Our vision is to create the most welcoming and efficient platform for team formation and management, where players of all skill levels can find their place and grow together. We're committed to fostering a positive environment where collaboration and mutual improvement are valued above all.

Community Guidelines

  • Alt Grinding will result in an account ban. The use of multiple accounts to farm points or resources is strictly prohibited and will lead to permanent suspension of all associated accounts.
  • Respect all community members. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated. This includes in-game chat, team communications, and all platform interactions.
  • No exploitation of game mechanics or bugs. Deliberately taking advantage of glitches, bugs, or unintended game mechanics to gain an unfair advantage is prohibited and may result in penalties including account suspension.
